Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.0 Date: 24th August 2025

At APPIFY APPS LLC, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your information through our services (referred to collectively as the "Services"). We are dedicated to protecting your privacy in accordance with the terms of this policy.

Compliance with Google API Services

We adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy and observe its Limited Use requirements when using information obtained from Google APIs. The specifics include:

  • Restricting human access to your data unless we receive your explicit consent.
  • Prohibiting the use of your data for advertising purposes such as retargeting, personalized, or interest-based ads.
  • Using the data solely to enhance or provide user-centric features visible in our application.
  • Allowing data transfer only when necessary to facilitate or improve user-centric features.

Data Retention and Deletion Policy

Our app does not retain any user data from Google services. We only temporarily access the data provided by Google during the user's active session. Once the user logs out, all information fetched from Google services is immediately discarded and is no longer stored or accessible within the app. We do not retain, store, or process any user data after the session ends.

Information Sharing with Third Party AI Platform

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. In accordance with this policy, we do not share any personal information with third-party AI platforms, with the sole exception of the prompts you input while utilizing write AI emails. For example, a prompt such as 'Request for meeting next week' may be shared. It is important to note that these prompts are processed in a manner that completely disassociates them from your identity, ensuring that they cannot be linked back to you or any specific individual. Additionally, we implement strict data security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of your data.

No Use of Google Workspace APIs for AI/ML Training

Our application does not use any data obtained from Google Workspace APIs to develop, improve, or train generalized artificial intelligence (AI) or machine learning (ML) models. We only access Google Workspace data temporarily during an active session to provide the requested service, and once the session ends, all data is discarded immediately.

Information Collection

We gather various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: This includes details like your name & email address which you provide when you register for our Services or interact with us.
  • Usage Information: We automatically collect information related to your interactions and usage of our Services, such as log data and device information.

Use of Information

  • To deliver and manage our Services, including account creation, payment processing, and communications regarding your account.
  • To customize your experience with our Services, offering tailored content and recommendations.
  • To improve and innovate our Services by analyzing trends and user behaviors.
  • To safeguard our rights and property and fulfill our legal responsibilities.

Information Sharing

We might share your information under these circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • With third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Services, such as hosting, payment processing, analytics and customer support.
  • In response to legal requests or when necessary to protect our rights, property, or the safety of our users and the public.

User Rights and Preferences

You can control how we use and share your information through several options:

  • "Limit Ad Tracking" on your iOS device in Settings / Privacy / Advertising. Please find additional information here: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202074
  • "Opt out of Ad Personalization" on your Android device in Settings / Google / Ads.
  • To opt-out of interest based advertising you can also visit: https://youradchoices.com

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no security system is infallible, and we cannot guarantee complete security of your data.

Policy Updates

We may modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal, technical, or business practices. Should there be a significant modification, we will notify you via email or through a notice on our website.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@appifyapps.com
